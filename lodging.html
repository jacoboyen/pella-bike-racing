Lodging

Hotels-Pella

Holiday Inn Express in Pella is the closest hotel to the race course. Call the hotel to reserve your room today: 641-628-4853.

Hotels-Knoxville

Cobblestone Hotel and Suites in Knoxville is close to all of the action and Peace Tree Brewery. Reserve your room today by calling 641-828-6100.

Camping

There are many campsites located close to the race course. The North Overlook campground is close to the action and has space for tent camping as well as full electric hookups. Ivan's campground is also close to the race course and has a nice overlook of the Des Moines River. For more information see the Army Corp website.
